DISCORD COMMUNITY BOTS

We operate bots in our official Discord server that integrate Discord with Auto Battles Online. If you use our Discord server, we may collect and process:

  • Discord account data: your Discord user ID and username, and (if you link your game account) the association between your Discord account and your in-game account.
  • Roles and activity: Discord role assignments we manage (e.g., in-game guild, arena rank, platform roles) and a timestamp of your last activity in the server, used to keep role synchronization current.
  • Bridged chat messages: messages you post in designated "world chat" bridge channels (clearly labeled in the channel description) are relayed into the game's World Chat, and are stored in our game database as part of chat history. Emojis, custom emotes, and links are removed before relay. Messages posted in other channels are not relayed or stored.

We use this data to provide account linking, in-game rewards for community participation, role synchronization, the Discord-to-game chat bridge, and community moderation. We do not sell this data or use it to train AI models. To unlink your Discord account or request deletion of this data, use the "Manage Data" option in the game's settings menu or contact us at autobattlesonline@gmail.com.
